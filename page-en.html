Tourism in Kazakhstan



Tourism in Kazakhstan, types, directions, problems and prospects of development Tourism in Kazakhstan is currently one of the actively developing industries, attracting significant interest among both local residents and foreign tourists.



In Almaty, tourism is under special attention of local authorities. Specialized companies and agencies have taken on the organization of tourist services, the development of new routes and their promotion. Kazakhstani websites play an important role in the popularization of tourism, and their number continues to grow.



What do local tour operators offer? Domestic tourism in Kazakhstan is actively expanding, but foreign tourists remain a priority for local authorities and travel agencies. These are primarily vacationers and guests from Russia, Belarus and other countries. Which destinations are open to travelers?



The main types of tourism in Kazakhstan are the following:


  • Cultural and historical
  • Wellness
  • Extreme
  • Ecological


Guests from different parts of the world are delighted and surprised to discover this amazing land, full of beautiful nature and many historical sights. For active tourists, a variety of active recreation is offered in Almaty: jeeping, yachting, mountain climbing and much more!


Ecotourism is becoming increasingly popular in Kazakhstan. Experts believe that this is the direction that requires active development. Of course, there are problems in Kazakhstan's tourism, and there are quite a few of them, but local authorities and businesses have chosen the right course for development.



Tourism in Almaty is a great alternative to European destinations, providing an opportunity to organize a quality vacation in a new place with unforgettable natural landscapes!
